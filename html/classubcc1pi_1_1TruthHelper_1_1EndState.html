enum ubcc1pi::TruthHelper::EndState::Type : unsigned int

The type of end state enumeration.

Enumerator
None 

The particle just stops after running out of energy.

DecayToMuon 

The particle decays to a muon (which could then in turn decay to a Michel)

InelasticAbsorption 

The particle interacts inelastically with a nucleus and is absorbed.

Pi0ChargeExchange 

The particle interacts inelastically with a nucleus and a Pi0 is in the final state.

Other 

None of the above.
