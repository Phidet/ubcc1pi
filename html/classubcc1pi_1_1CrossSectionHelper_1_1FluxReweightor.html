Detailed Description

The flux reweightor class. Used to calculate the flux in a given systematic universe.

The flux is defined as a function of the true neutrino energy. Here we get the total event rate as a function of neutrino energy for the nominal universe and universes with a varied flux. The ratio of the event rate in each energy bin (varied/nominal) is used to scale the nominal flux to give the flux distribution in the varied universe.
