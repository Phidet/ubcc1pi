typedef std::tuple< bool, std::vector<std::string>, std::vector<int> > ubcc1pi::SelectionHelper::EventSelection::SelectionResult

The selection result type definition. A tuple of three entries: 0. bool = if the event passed the last cut of the selection.

  1. vector<string> = the names of the cuts that the selection passed (even if it didn't pass the last cut)
  2. vector<int> = one entry per reco particle, the PDG codes that were assigned by the selection
