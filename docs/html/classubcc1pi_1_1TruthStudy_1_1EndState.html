enum ubcc1pi::TruthStudy::EndState::Type

The type of end state enumeration.

Enumerator:
None 

The pion just stops after running out of energy.

DecayToMuon 

The pion decays to a muon (which could then in turn decay to a Michel).

InelasticAbsorption 

The pion interacts inelastically with a nucleus and is absorbed.

Pi0ChargeExchange 

The pion interacts inelastically with a nucleus and a Pi0 is in the final state.

Other 

None of the above.
